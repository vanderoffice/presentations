We chose the Little Hoover Commission model deliberately. Cross-branch appointments create legitimacy that executive-only governance lacks. The judicial council needs data for court operations. The legislature needs data for oversight. An independent office with staggered terms survives administration transitions‚Äîwhich is essential for multi-year modernization.
